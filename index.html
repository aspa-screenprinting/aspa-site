What the Exam Covers

01
General Screen Printing

History, terminology, equipment setup, safety, and workflow fundamentals.

02
Graphics & Art Fundamentals

CMYK color theory, color separations, halftones, and artwork preparation.

03
Textile Printing

Plastisol and water-based inks, curing temperatures, garment types, and print techniques.

04
Non-Textile & Industrial

Signage, decals, specialty substrates, UV inks, and industrial applications.
