4CSP Certification Exam

Exam Details

The Certified Screen Printer (CSP) examination is administered by ASPA as a comprehensive assessment of professional screen printing knowledge and competency. Exam details are as follows:

  • Examination Fee: $59 per attempt
  • Number of Questions: 36 multiple-choice questions
  • Passing Score: 80% (approximately 29 of 36 correct answers)
  • Format: Open-book examination (official ASPA study guide permitted)
  • Duration: 90 minutes

Examination Policies

No Refunds: Examination fees are non-refundable once the exam has been started. If you need to cancel prior to starting the exam, contact ASPA within 24 hours of registration for a potential refund.

Study Materials: Candidates may reference the official ASPA CSP Study Guide during the examination. Unauthorized materials, external devices (except for basic calculators), or assistance from other individuals is strictly prohibited.

Certificate Issuance: Candidates who achieve a passing score receive the CSP credential and official certificate within 5 business days. Certificates are valid for three (3) years from the date of issuance.

Recertification: ASPA+ members (Professional or Business tier) are eligible for recertification renewal at a reduced rate within 90 days of credential expiration. Non-members and expired credentials require re-examination at full examination fee.
