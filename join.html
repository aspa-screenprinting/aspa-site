COMMON QUESTIONS

How does annual billing work?

ASPA+ memberships renew annually. You'll receive a reminder before each renewal. Cancel anytime — your benefits remain active through the end of your paid period.

I'm a Legacy member. What changes for me?

Nothing! Your existing membership and all benefits are fully grandfathered. You can optionally upgrade to ASPA+ at a discounted rate to unlock new perks like discounts, CE credits, and priority placement.

What are CE credits?

Continuing Education credits track your professional development. ASPA+ members earn CE credits through webinars, training, and annual recertification — demonstrating ongoing expertise to clients.

Do I need a membership to take the CSP exam?

No. The CSP exam ($59) is available to anyone. However, ASPA+ Business members get annual recertification included and the "ASPA Certified" badge on their directory listing.

How quickly will my listing go live?

Business and Supplier listings are typically activated within 24-48 hours of enrollment. ASPA+ members receive priority placement in directory search results.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Discover, Amex) and PayPal. Annual billing is automatic with easy cancellation.
