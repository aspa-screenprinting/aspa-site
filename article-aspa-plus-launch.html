ASPA Launches ASPA+ Membership: What's New for 2026

Published March 2026 · 6 min read

Whether you're a home-based printer, a production shop, or an industry supplier, ASPA+ has a tier built for you. Here's everything you need to know.

Introduction: A New Chapter for ASPA

The American Screen Printing Association is evolving. In 2026, ASPA is launching ASPA+ — a new annual membership model designed to give screen printing professionals ongoing value, not just a one-time transaction. Whether you're a home-based printer, a production shop, or an industry supplier, ASPA+ has a tier built for you.

This launch represents a fundamental shift in how we serve the screen printing community. Instead of a static, one-time membership purchase, ASPA+ delivers continuous benefits: fresh educational resources every month, evolving tools that grow with your business, expanded industry networking opportunities, and exclusive partnerships that directly impact your bottom line.

For nearly 23 years, ASPA has been the home base for screen printing professionals worldwide. ASPA+ is our commitment to remaining the most valuable resource in the industry — one where membership actively supports your growth, year after year.

Why the Change?

ASPA has served the screen printing community since 2004 with members in over 100 countries. The original membership model was one-time payment, lifetime access — and that worked for years. But the industry evolves fast, and members told us they want more: ongoing education, fresh resources, industry connections, and tools that grow with their businesses. ASPA+ is the answer — a sustainable model that lets us continuously invest in new content, tools, and community features for our members.

What About Existing Members?

Here's the most important part: Legacy members (existing one-time purchases) are fully grandfathered — nothing changes for them. They keep all current benefits indefinitely. They can upgrade to ASPA+ at a special discounted rate to unlock new perks, but there's zero pressure. Your existing membership remains valid forever.

This is our way of honoring the professionals who believed in ASPA from the beginning. You're the foundation of this community, and we're not abandoning that trust.

Three ASPA+ Tiers

ASPA+ is designed for different needs across the screen printing ecosystem. Choose the tier that fits your role and business stage.

ASPA+ Business ($49/year)

For screen printing shops, t-shirt businesses, and individual printers.

  • Priority placement in the Member Directory (ASPA+ members appear first in search results)
  • Annual CSP recertification included (no separate recert fee)
  • Continuing Education (CE) credits through ASPA content and events
  • Industry supplier discount program (exclusive deals on ink, equipment, blanks)
  • Access to exclusive webinars and educational resources
  • ASPA+ digital badge for your website and marketing
  • Monthly member newsletter with industry insights

ASPA+ Supplier ($99/year)

For equipment manufacturers, ink suppliers, blank garment distributors, and service providers.

  • Featured supplier spotlight in the ASPA directory
  • Promotion to ASPA members via newsletter and resource hub
  • Member-to-supplier deal promotion platform
  • Logo placement in ASPA publications and events
  • Access to industry data and member demographics
  • All Business tier benefits included

ASPA+ Training ($79/year)

For educators, schools, and training programs.

  • Updated training content and new modules released annually
  • CE credits for instructors
  • Curriculum alignment with CSP exam content
  • Student discount on CSP exam fees
  • Access to educational resources and teaching guides
  • All Business tier benefits included
Pro Tip: Not sure which tier fits your situation? The Business tier is perfect for individual printers and small shops. Suppliers should go Supplier. If you run a training program, the Training tier gives you curriculum alignment and student discounts that justify the investment immediately.

Continuing Education: A First for Screen Printing

One of the biggest additions in ASPA+: a structured CE credit system. This is genuinely new for the screen printing industry.

CSP-certified professionals will earn credits through ASPA content consumption, webinar attendance, training completion, and industry participation. CE credits keep your certification active and ensure you're staying current with evolving techniques, safety standards, and industry trends.

Why does this matter? In other professional trades—plumbing, electrical work, healthcare—continuing education is the standard. It signals to clients and employers that you're serious about mastery. ASPA+ brings that same professional standard to screen printing.

How CE Credits Work

  • Read an ASPA educational article (15 min): 0.25 credits
  • Watch a training webinar (1 hour): 1 credit
  • Complete a full course module: 2-4 credits
  • Attend an ASPA industry event (half-day): 2 credits
  • Participate in community forums and peer learning: Bonus credits

Your credit tracking dashboard shows your progress toward renewal requirements. No complex forms. No confusion. Just transparent, trackable professional development.

The Supplier Discount Program

ASPA+ members get access to negotiated discounts with leading industry suppliers. We're building partnerships with equipment manufacturers, ink companies, blank garment distributors, and software providers to bring exclusive pricing to the ASPA community.

Details on launch partners will be announced soon — but the goal is simple: your ASPA+ membership should pay for itself in supplier savings within the first few orders.

Why This Works

The screen printing supply chain is fragmented. You buy ink from one vendor, equipment from another, blanks from a third, software from a fourth. ASPA+ aggregates member buying power across 1,200+ professionals, giving us leverage to negotiate better pricing.

Those savings flow directly to you. A shop that orders supplies monthly could save $300-$1,000 annually just through the discount program. That's 6-20 times the cost of a $49 membership.

What Stays the Same

Important: ASPA's core offerings aren't changing. We're not gatekeeping existing resources or charging for things that were free before.

  • The CSP certification exam remains available to everyone ($59) — no paywall, no membership required
  • The Resource Hub and educational articles remain free and public — anyone can read, learn, and grow
  • The Member Directory remains accessible to the public — it helps your customers find you!
  • ASPA's mission — Education, Certification, Community — is unchanged
  • Legacy members retain all existing benefits

ASPA+ is additive — it adds new value on top of what already exists. Nothing is being taken away.

The Future of ASPA

ASPA+ is just the beginning. In 2026 and beyond, we're investing in:

  • New training modules and online courses — video-based, hands-on learning from industry experts
  • Expanded Resource Hub with monthly new content — trending techniques, industry news, case studies
  • Member community features — forums, live networking events, regional meetups
  • Industry reports and trend analysis — data-driven insights about market demand, supply chain changes, and emerging techniques
  • A job board and marketplace — connect talent with opportunities, buy and sell used equipment
  • Specialty certification tracks beyond the general CSP — focused credentials in areas like discharge printing, plastisol foil, or digital/hybrid methods
  • Partnerships with industry events and organizations — making ASPA the hub of professional screen printing

Screen printing is a $10+ billion global industry — and ASPA is committed to being the home base for professionals who take their craft seriously.

Join us. Whether you're printing one shirt a week or running a multi-million dollar operation, your growth matters to us. ASPA+ is built around your success.
