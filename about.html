ABOUT ASPA

The American Screen Printing Association — serving the global screen printing community since 2004.

Who We Are

ASPA is a privately held corporation founded in 2004, dedicated to serving the global screen printing industry. We provide comprehensive information on screen printing technology, training programs, and professional certification for industry professionals.

Our community includes thousands of members worldwide — certified screen printers, embroidery shops, sign shops, graphic artists, equipment suppliers, and manufacturers. Members represent over 100 countries, and our platform serves millions of page views annually.

ASPA Institute

Online Education for Screen Printing

The ASPA Institute is the education division of ASPA, dedicated to making professional screen printing education accessible worldwide.

Diploma in Screen Printing Course — Created in September 2013, our comprehensive online training has enrolled hundreds of students throughout the US and worldwide.
